# Diala Abedalqader

## About

I am an AI/ML engineer and researcher based at the **University of Sussex**. My work sits at the intersection of applied machine learning and mathematical foundations — I care about understanding *why* models work, not just making them work.

I focus on three areas:

- **Natural Language Processing** — text understanding, language models, and semantic systems
- **Computer Vision** — image recognition, visual reasoning, and multimodal learning
- **Data Engineering** — building robust pipelines that make ML systems reliable in production

My research interests extend into the mathematical structures that underpin modern deep learning: linear algebra, probability theory, optimization, and information geometry. I believe rigorous mathematical thinking leads to better engineering decisions.
